Now, in my Master of Business Information Technology at RMIT Melbourne, I'm pairing that domain depth with modern BI tooling — SQL, Power BI, Tableau and Azure — across nine projects spanning analysis, intelligence, delivery and risk. I like problems where understanding the business and understanding the data are the same job.

Outside the work, I mentor postgraduate peers at RMIT and help run student analytics and finance communities.
