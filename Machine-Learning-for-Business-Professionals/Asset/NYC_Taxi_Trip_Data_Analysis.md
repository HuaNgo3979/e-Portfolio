# NYC Taxi Trip Data Analysis

## Organisation Overview

New York City's taxi service is an essential part of the urban transportation network, providing quick and efficient transportation solutions across the city. The NYC Taxi & Limousine Commission (TLC) regulates this vital service, ensuring safety and compliance while collecting comprehensive trip data to improve and innovate the system continually.

## Business Challenges

The taxi industry faces many challenges, worsened by changing urban dynamics and technological disruptions. Key challenges include:

- Traditional taxis are increasingly losing market share to app-based ride-sharing services, which are more competitively priced and offer greater convenience.

- Struggling to maintain high service quality amidst stiff competition and varying customer expectations.

- Frequently facing mismatches between the number of available taxis and the demand, across different city areas and times.

- Challenges in optimizing route and fare calculations to effectively maximize earnings and minimize idle times.

- Difficulties in leveraging extensive trip data to effectively enhance service and operational strategies.

## Vision

- Implement systems to analyze data in real-time to provide live insights into demand and supply dynamics, enabling immediate adjustments to fleet deployment and routing.

- Utilize predictive analytics to anticipate future trends and needs, such as predicting peak hours and popular destinations, allowing for proactive resource allocation.

- Surge pricing incentivizes operation in high-demand areas during peak hours.

- Achieve and maintain the status as the preferred taxi service in New York City through innovation, superior service quality, and robust operational tactics.

- Improve customer satisfaction.

To overcome these challenges and meet their vision, New York City's taxi service needs to implement an intelligent enterprise system to make timely and informed decisions. These initiatives will position NYC taxis as a more responsive, efficient, and customer-focused service within the urban transport network.
